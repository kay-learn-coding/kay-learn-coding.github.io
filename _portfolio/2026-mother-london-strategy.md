## Executive Summary
This project evaluates the advertising agency **Mother London** at a critical industry junction where the market is bifurcated by the tension of **Human vs. AI**. While Mother's 100% independent model remains its primary competitive differentiator, accelerating structural shifts require an immediate strategic evolution.

### Key Frameworks & Methodologies Demonstrated
* **Macro & Micro Environmental Auditing:** Evaluated regulatory liabilities under the **UK DMCC Act** (penalties up to 10% of global turnover) and the CMA Green Claims Code.
* **Next-Generation Digital Architecture:** Formulated strategies leveraging **Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO)** to ensure creative brand assets are perfectly machine-readable and highly indexed by Large Language Models (LLMs).
* **Human-Centric Measurement Systems:** Pioneered value-based marketing KPIs, moving away from superficial engagement metrics to track **Share of Model (SOM)** and **Share of Search (SOS)**.
